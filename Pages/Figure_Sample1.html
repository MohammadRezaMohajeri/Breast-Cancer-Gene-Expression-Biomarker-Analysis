Gene Set Enrichment Analysis (GSEA) is a widely used method for discovering the underlying biological processes in clinical samples by analyzing their gene expression patterns. It assesses the enrichment of annotated gene sets, which represent specific biological processes, among genes that are differentially expressed in these samples. GSEA helps identify and understand the biological pathways and functions that are associated with the observed gene expression changes in clinical contexts.
